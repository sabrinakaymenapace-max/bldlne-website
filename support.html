Support

For account help, billing questions, verification issues, or general inquiries about BLDLNE, contact our support team:

support@bldlne.app

If you use the BLDLNE mobile app while signed in, you can submit a report from your profile to send your account details to support automatically.

What to include

To help us respond faster, please include:

  • Your account email address.
  • A clear description of your question or issue.
  • Screenshots or relevant details for billing, verification, or profile problems.

Billing support

Subscription payments are processed through Stripe. For billing questions, subscription cancellations, or payment receipt requests, email us at the address above and include the email used for your BLDLNE account and Stripe checkout.

Response times

We aim to respond to support requests within a reasonable timeframe, typically within a few business days.

Account deletion

To request deletion of your account or personal information, see our Delete account page.
