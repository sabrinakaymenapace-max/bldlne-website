Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how BLDLNE (“BLDLNE,” “we,” “us”) collects, uses, and shares information when you use our website at https://bldlne.app and our mobile application (together, the “Service”).

Information we collect

We collect information you provide directly, information generated through your use of the Service, and limited technical data needed to operate the platform.

  • Account information such as your name, email address, phone number, and login credentials.
  • Breeder profile information such as kennel name, location, bio, photos, videos, dog listings, litter details, and puppy availability.
  • Uploaded documents and media, including health records, registration details, pedigree information, and identity verification materials submitted for review.
  • Communications sent through the platform, including messages between breeders and dog seekers.
  • Basic usage and analytics data, such as pages viewed, feature interactions, device type, and general app performance information.

How we use information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve the Service.
  • Create and manage user and breeder accounts.
  • Display breeder profiles, dogs, litters, and related content to other users.
  • Process subscriptions and billing through Stripe.
  • Support verification, moderation, and trust-related features.
  • Respond to support requests and communicate about the Service.
  • Monitor security, prevent abuse, and comply with legal obligations.

Payment processing

Breeder subscriptions are billed through Stripe. When you subscribe, payment information is collected and processed directly by Stripe. BLDLNE does not store full payment card numbers on our servers. Stripe’s handling of payment data is governed by Stripe’s own privacy policy and terms.

How we share information

We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With other users, when you publish a breeder profile, dog listing, litter, or other public content on the platform.
  • With service providers that help us operate the Service, such as hosting, analytics, identity verification, and payment processing partners like Stripe.
  • When required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, and integrity of BLDLNE and its users.
  • In connection with a business transaction such as a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

Data retention

We retain information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. You may request account-related assistance by contacting us.

Your choices

You can update certain profile information within the app. You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where applicable. Some information may need to be retained for legal, security, or operational reasons.

To request account deletion, see our Delete account page.

Security

We use reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational measures designed to protect information. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children

BLDLNE is not intended for children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the Service after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
