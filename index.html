Transparency

Breeder Transparency

Know who you're dealing with. Breeders who earn the Identity Verified badge have completed identity verification, including government ID verification, phone verification, and email verification. This helps reduce fake accounts, anonymous sellers, and common online scams by confirming that a real, accountable person is behind the profile. While identity verification does not guarantee breeding quality or ethical practices, it provides an additional layer of trust and accountability for buyers.

Dog & Puppy Transparency

Know what has been verified. Dogs and puppies displaying the Verified badge have been confirmed through BLDLNE's verification process. Breeders must upload a new photo showing the animal alongside a unique BLDLNE verification code generated by the platform. This helps reduce fake listings, stolen photos, and scams involving non-existent animals. Every verified listing also displays a Last Verified date so buyers can see how recently the dog or puppy was confirmed to exist. Verification confirms the animal was present at the time of verification but does not guarantee health, pedigree accuracy, temperament, or future availability.
